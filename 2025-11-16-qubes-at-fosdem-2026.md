The Qubes OS Project [will have a stand](https://fosdem.org/2026/news/2025-11-16-accepted-stands/) at [FOSDEM 2026](https://fosdem.org/2026/), which will take place in Brussels, Belgium on January 31 and February 1, 2026. FOSDEM is a top annual meeting for free and open source software developers. Entry is free, and no registration is required. If you attend, stop by and say hello!
